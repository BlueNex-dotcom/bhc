What We Offer

We source properties across the South West including Devon, Cornwall and Somerset, which are then purchased by ourselves or by our investors below market value. These are then renovated before being resold or rented out to letting agencies or HMOs/SAs. Overall we cover a range of property investment project such as:

  • Sourcing and joint venture opportunities
  • Light refurbishment projects
  • Commercial to residential conversion projects
  • HMO & SA property options
  • Assisted selling for those looking to sell fast
